Hydrogen (H)

The simplest atom — 1 proton, no neutrons. Makes up most of the universe.

Subatomic particles

Proton — charge +1, mass 1  (in nucleus)
Neutron — charge 0, mass 1  (in nucleus)
Electron — charge −1, tiny mass  (in shells)
Atomic number Z = protons
Mass number A = protons + neutrons
Neutrons = A − Z
